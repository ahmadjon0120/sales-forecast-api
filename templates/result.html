Understanding the Results

Term Explanation
ds The specific date for the forecast.
Forecast (yhat) The model's single best guess for the sales quantity on that date.
Pessimistic Estimate (yhat_lower) The lower boundary of the forecast. The model is confident the actual value will not be lower than this.
Optimistic Estimate (yhat_upper) The upper boundary of the forecast. The model is confident the actual value will not be higher than this.
